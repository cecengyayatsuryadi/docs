# Blameless Incident Reviews

> Team-level learning from incidents. Focus on **systems and processes**, not individuals.

**Last updated:** YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Principles

1. **No blame** — we review systems, not people
2. **Curiosity over judgment** — "how did this make sense at the time?"
3. **Fix the system** — the goal is preventing recurrence, not assigning fault
4. **Share openly** — findings are shared with the team, not hidden

---

## When to Conduct a Review

- After any Severity 1 or 2 incident
- After a rollback
- After data loss or corruption
- When an incident reveals a systemic process gap
