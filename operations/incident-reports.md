# Incident Reports

> Timeline and impact documentation for incidents. Focus on **what happened**, not blame.

**Template:** [templates/incident-report-template.md](../templates/incident-report-template.md)

---

## When to Add an Entry

- Service is down or degraded for users
- Data is corrupted or lost
- A deployment fails and requires rollback
- Security vulnerability is exploited
